Biography

Eduard Saller is part of an Industrial PhD program at the Center for Information and Language Processing of the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich and the BMW GROUP. He is under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hinrich Schütze and Dr. Benjamin Roth.

His research topics include question answering systems, knowledge graphs and memory networks.

He is also part of a BMW GROUP InnovationLab located in Munich, Germany, which develops vision based (object detection, object classification) and language based (information extraction, knowledge graph, question answering) systems.
